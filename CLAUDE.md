# 1. Project Identity

**GetKonduit Webpage** is a portfolio and showcase site for GetKonduit, an automation business. The goal is to give prospects a clear view of what GetKonduit does and make it easy to reach out. Success = a live, professional site where visitors understand the value proposition and know how to contact us.
